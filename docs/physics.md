# Physics Model

## Binary Black Hole Inspiral

The simulation represents two compact objects orbiting their common center of mass.

The orbital separation gradually decreases until merger.

---

## Event Horizon

Each black hole is represented by an event horizon whose apparent radius scales with mass.

---

## Accretion Disk

Particles orbit each black hole while gradually spiraling inward.

The visualization is physically inspired rather than numerically exact.

---

## Gravitational Waves

The telemetry waveform illustrates the characteristic chirp expected during inspiral.

Amplitude and frequency increase rapidly before merger.

---

## Ringdown

After merger the final black hole settles into equilibrium while the emitted waveform decays.
